Hair Loss 101

Understanding the biology of
Androgenetic Alopecia.

The Shrinking Follicle

It starts microscopically. Genetic sensitivity to hormones causes follicles to miniaturize. Over time, they produce thinner, shorter hairs until they cease production entirely.

  • Growth Phase (Anagen) Shortens
    Hair doesn't reach full length.
  • Resting Phase (Telogen) Lengthens
    More empty follicles at any given time.
